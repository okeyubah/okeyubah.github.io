This website was set up in order to teach Nigerian teenagers Java, starting from SS1 (Senior Secondary 1).

To teach Java, we use "A Peek into Java for Nigerian Teenagers", a book that was recently approved by the Lagos State Ministry of Education, for use in senior secondary schools. The author of that book, Anthony Okechukwu Ubah, also happens to be the owner of this blog.

In short, this website was created in order to help the author of "A Peek into Java for Nigerian Teenagers" explain the contents of his book to as many teenagers as possible.

The objective here is to help interested pupils overcome the initial obstacle posed by the kind of jargon employed by computer programmers. The writer of this blog is convinced that, by starting early, our teenagers stand a better chance of mastering the kind of language that is often referred to as code.

To get started, click the Staff link below, then select the posts that were written by this author. You can also click the Topics link below, to select any topic from the book.
