Starting from SS1 (Senior Secondary 1), pupils can explore:

A Peek into Java for Nigerian Teenagers

This book, written by the owner of this blog,

  • was recently approved by the Lagos State Ministry of Education
  • will be explained in great detail by its author, Anthony Okechukwu Ubah
  • could eventually lead the pupil to a lucrative role as a computer programmer

The objective:

  • ensure that pupils understand the cryptic words that help to mystify the language of computer programmers
  • take pupils slowly but assuredly through the fundamental aspects of the Java programming language
  • convince parents that this should be an important part of their children's curriculum
